# 📁 Assets Upload Guide — Mubashshir's Portfolio

Drop your media files into the correct folder. They will appear automatically in the **Creative Showcase** section of the website.

---

## 📸 Photos

| Folder | What to upload |
|--------|---------------|
| `photos/random/` | General / random pictures |
| `photos/community/` | Community Engagement photos |
| `photos/community-global/` | Community Engagement – Global Citizen photos |

**Supported formats:** `.jpg` `.jpeg` `.png` `.gif` `.webp`

---

## 🎬 Videos

| Folder | What to upload |
|--------|---------------|
| `videos/promotional/` | Promotional Video – Advertisement |
| `videos/convocation/` | Convocation Live footage |
| `videos/journalism/` | Broadcast Journalism clips |
| `videos/documentary/` | 24 Minutes Documentary |
| `videos/short-film/` | 7 Minutes Short Film |

**Supported formats:** `.mp4` `.mov` `.webm` `.avi`

---

## 🖼️ Thumbnails (for Videos)

Place a thumbnail image for a video inside `thumbnails/` with the **same filename** but `.jpg` extension.

**Example:** If your video is `videos/documentary/lanoh.mp4`, put its thumbnail at `thumbnails/lanoh.jpg`.

---

## ✅ Tips

- Keep filenames clean: use hyphens instead of spaces (`my-photo.jpg` not `my photo.jpg`)
- Resize large images to max 2000px wide before uploading for faster loading
- For videos, use `.mp4` format for best browser compatibility
